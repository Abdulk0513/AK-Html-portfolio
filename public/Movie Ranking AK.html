The Best Movies According to AK

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Directed by Frank Darabont, this film follows the journey of a banker wrongly convicted of murder as he forms unlikely friendships and orchestrates a daring escape from Shawshank State Penitentiary.

The Godfather (1972)

Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this iconic crime drama explores the Corleone family's patriarch, Vito Corleone, and his son Michael's reluctant rise to power in the New York Mafia underworld.

Inception (2010)

Directed by Christopher Nolan, this mind-bending thriller follows a skilled thief who enters the subconscious of his targets to steal valuable information, but is tasked with a seemingly impossible mission of planting an idea instead.
